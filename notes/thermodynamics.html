Thermodynamics

First Law of Thermodynamics

The First Law states that energy in a closed system is conserved. It can be written as ΔE = Q - W.

Second Law of Thermodynamics

Heat cannot spontaneously flow from a colder body to a hotter body. Entropy always increases in an isolated system.
